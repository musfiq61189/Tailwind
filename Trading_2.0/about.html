HEALTH SAFETY &

ENVIRONMENT

Welcome to Utshob Trading , where good health meets good food. At Utshob Trading , we believe that a strong safety culture is paramount to delivering quality products to our valued customers. Our commitment to safety is reflected in our meticulous approach towards creating a secure and healthy work environment for our associates. Under the umbrella of Utshob Trading , our subsidiary, takes pride in upholding industry-leading safety standards. With a highly skilled management team at the helm, Technic embraces a robust safety culture that sets the benchmark in the food trading industry. At the core of our safety measures is our specialized Health & Safety team, dedicated to ensuring a safe and healthy workplace for all personnel involved in our operations. The well-being of our associates is our top priority, and we go the extra mile to implement maximum safety precautions at our projects and workplaces.

SUPPLIERS OF

OUR OPERATIONS

At the heart of our seamless operations lies a robust network of trusted suppliers, each carefully selected to uphold the standards of excellence we are committed to delivering. Our suppliers play a pivotal role in ensuring the quality, reliability, and sustainability of the products that define our brand.
1. Quality Assurance: Our suppliers are chosen based on stringent quality assurance criteria. We prioritize partners who share our dedication to delivering products of the highest standard, meeting and often exceeding the expectations of our discerning customers.
2. Sustainable Practices: Environmental responsibility is a cornerstone of our operations. We collaborate with suppliers who embrace sustainable practices, contributing to the reduction of our ecological footprint. From sourcing raw materials to packaging, our suppliers align with our commitment to a greener future.
3. Reliability and Timeliness: Timely delivery is crucial to maintaining the efficiency of our operations. Our suppliers are selected not only for the quality of their products but also for their reliability in meeting deadlines. This ensures that our customers receive their orders promptly, without compromise..
