Our Vision

Becoming industry benchmark leaders in the foodstuff trading sector aligning with the Bangladesh 2030 vision and extending beyond.

Our Mission

Achieving the founders' vision by sourcing top-quality foodstuff to meet demands and expanding the food service industry, catering to wholesalers and retailers. This effort is aimed at generating favorable returns for owners, benefiting society, and supporting our employees.

Our History

Originating from its base in Dhaka, Bangladesh, the company was established and launched, initiating operations and expanding to emerge as one of the premier private enterprises in food import and trade within Bangladesh.

About Us

Presently, the company efficiently conveys and disseminates food products across the entire Kingdom market utilizing an extensive fleet of well-equipped vehicles and a proficient sales team. This strategic approach has positioned the company as a frontrunner in the food industry. Engaging in the marketing and distribution of renowned brands, alongside its proprietary brands, has solidified its leadership in key Bangladeshi markets. The company's primary business operations are concentrated in the Central, Eastern, and Western regions, followed by other areas. Regarding suppliers, we maintain successful partnerships with leading international players in Europe, America, and Arab Mediterranean countries.
